Our Story

Wood Home Preschool was founded in 1995 by Jane Wood, a passionate educator with over 20 years of experience in early childhood education. What began as a small home-based preschool has grown into a beloved institution in our community.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can develop a lifelong love of learning. We believe that each child is unique and deserves an education tailored to their individual needs and interests.

Over the years, we have helped hundreds of children build strong foundations for future academic success while fostering social skills, creativity, and emotional intelligence.

Our Learning Philosophy

Woodhome Preschool nurtures early development through sensory-rich experiences that build healthy habits, strong relationships, and lifelong learning skills.
