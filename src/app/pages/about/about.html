Our Story

Founded over 25 years ago, we have grown to be a beloved institution in the community. Our mission is to be the launching point for children to develop an early love for learning. We focus on building confidence in our little learners, so they reach their highest potential with an experience tailored to their individual needs. Woodhome Preschool Center provides the perfect balance between social/emotional development and academic growth.

Over the years, Woodhome Preschool Center has been the start of the educational journey for many children as we provide a strong foundation for future success while fostering social skills, creativity, and academic readiness.
