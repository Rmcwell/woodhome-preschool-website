Our Magical Story 📚

Once upon a time in 1995, Miss Jane Wood had a wonderful dream - to create a special place where little ones could learn, play, and grow together! What started as a cozy home-based preschool has blossomed into a magical learning wonderland that our whole community loves.

Our mission is simple: to create a warm, happy place where every child feels loved and excited to discover new things every single day. We believe each little one is absolutely unique and special, deserving of their own personalized adventure in learning!

Over the years, we've watched hundreds of tiny tots grow into confident, curious, and kind children ready to take on the world. It's pure magic! ✨

Our Learning Philosophy

Woodhome Preschool nurtures early development through sensory-rich experiences that build healthy habits, strong relationships, and lifelong learning skills.

💡

Creative Thinking

We encourage children to think for themselves in creative ways, fostering imagination and independent problem-solving skills that will serve them throughout their lives.

🤝

Working Together

Our programs help children learn to work cooperatively with other children and adults, building essential social skills and teamwork abilities.

📚

Academic Foundation

Our curriculum helps children grasp integral early academic learning concepts, building a strong foundation for future educational success.
