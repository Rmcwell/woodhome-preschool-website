Inclement Weather Policy

Woodhome Preschool Center follows Baltimore County Public School (BCPS) closures, within reason.

  • If BCPS closes: NO programming.
  • If BCPS opens 1 hour late: NO before care; school opens at regular time.
  • If BCPS opens 2 hours late: NO before care or morning school; we will open at 12pm.
  • If BCPS closes 2 hours early: It is at the discretion of Woodhome Preschool Center to close school early.
  • The school calendar allows for up to 4 full snow days.

There are some situations where Woodhome Preschool Center will open even when BCPS is not open. Refer to school-wide communication channels for updates on openings and closures.
