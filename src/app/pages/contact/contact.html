Parent Resources

Payment

  • Registration fee payments must be made in full.
  • Tuition payments will be accepted in full at a 5% discount or in 9 monthly installments on the 1st of each month.
    The first installment is due September 1st. A $25 late fee will apply to payments received after the 7th of each month.
  • Before and Aftercare is billed separate from school on an hourly basis at $10 an hour.

Weather Policy

Woodhome Preschool Center follows Baltimore County Public School closures:

  • If BCPS is closed, there will be NO programming.
  • If BCPS is open 1 our late, we will have Preschool at regular class time.
  • If BCPS is 2 hours late, there will be NO morning school and we will open at 12:30pm.
  • If BCPS closes 2 hours early, it is to the discrection of the school to close early.
